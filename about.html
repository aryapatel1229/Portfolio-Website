About me

My entry into User Experience

My journey into User Experience (UX) began unexpectedly when I was talking to my cousin. She told me one day to look into UX as I enjoy creative side of business, and eventually this is something that I fell in love with.

What I find most appealing about being a UX Designer includes...

  • Utilizing both creative and analytical skills
  • Engaging with and designing for people
  • Exploring innovative ideas and creative solutions
  • Constantly seeking to make improvements

After exploring self-study routes and several online videos and readings, I enrolled in CareerFoundry's User Experience Immersive Course. This program helped me build and refine my UX skillset through project-based learning
